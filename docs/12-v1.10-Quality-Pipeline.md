# v1.10 – Quality Pipeline

The elevation-derived asset pipeline now uses a configurable Gaussian prefilter and real-world metre-per-pixel gradients.

Generated maps:

- `earth_normal.png` – geodetically scaled tangent-space normals
- `earth_slope.png` – slope angle normalized by `slopeReferenceDegrees`
- `earth_curvature.png` – stable curvature from filtered elevation
- `earth_ambient_occlusion.png` – multi-direction horizon occlusion

Diagnostics now split the packed material map into water, vegetation, desert and ice previews, and split the normal map into X, Y and Z channels.

The original NOAA GeoTIFF may still print harmless LibTiff.NET warnings for GeoTIFF/GDAL extension tags. Pixel data, tiling and Float32 decoding are unaffected.
